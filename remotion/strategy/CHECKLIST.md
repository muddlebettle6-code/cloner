# Reusable Reel Quality Checklist

Run before every render and before publishing. A reel ships only when every box is honestly checked.

## Story & hook
- [ ] Story has personal stakes + surprise + a clear visual + sourced data.
- [ ] 12 hooks generated; chosen one is the strongest **truthful** (not most sensational), clickbait risk low.
- [ ] First frame legible with no in-animation blur; subject understood in <2s.
- [ ] Hook makes a promise the reel actually delivers.

## Structure & length
- [ ] Retention arc present (stop → curiosity → … → payoff → forward close); not a reused template.
- [ ] Shortest length that delivers context→evidence→consequence→payoff (target 20–45s).
- [ ] Pace contrast: fast setup, a still beat before the key number, slower payoff.
- [ ] Payoff resolves the hook with a *useful* takeaway; ending isn't a question-as-crutch.
- [ ] Loop considered (final frame ties to opening) where it doesn't hurt the conclusion.

## Motion & visuals (anti-repetition)
- [ ] Checked `VISUAL_MEMORY.md`: main object / transition / camera / chart / SFX not repeated from last reel.
- [ ] Within-beat motion on most beats (counter, draw, sweep, reveal, flip) — info moves, not just appears.
- [ ] At least several original visual ideas this reel; brand constants kept (black/magenta/white, Neue, mark).
- [ ] Magenta used selectively (emphasis), not everywhere.

## Captions
- [ ] Verbatim, word-synced, one phrase group on screen, numbers in magenta.
- [ ] Inside the central safe zone; clear of charts and the bottom UI; enough read time.

## Voice & sound
- [ ] Voice chosen for fit (not defaulted to deepest); tested ≥2 on the hook.
- [ ] SFX story-matched; no repeated main impact/whoosh/riser from the last reel.
- [ ] Hierarchy: narration > key SFX > music (off by default) > ambience. Checked on phone + laptop speakers.

## News integrity
- [ ] Every number traces to a saved/cited source; estimates labeled as estimates (e.g., "effective").
- [ ] Framing fair; visuals imply nothing unsupported; no fabricated figures; not investment advice.

## Platform / export
- [ ] 1080×1920, 9:16, 30fps, H.264 High + AAC, ~10–12 Mbps, faststart.
- [ ] Grain/dither added (kills magenta-glow banding); thick lines, big labels, strong contrast.
- [ ] No watermark; original content; no engagement bait.
- [ ] Cover: subject + headline central (grid center-crop); 3 variants reviewed at thumbnail size.
- [ ] Separate IG vs FB captions; "Upload at highest quality" reminder noted.

## Self-critique (5 lenses) done
- [ ] Scroller, Retention editor, Motion designer, News editor, Platform reviewer all passed; blocking issues fixed; re-rendered.
